Japan Trip!

Hello! My name Julia, a sophomore at studying Data Science and Finance at Northeastern University! I'm orginally from the Bay Area, California. This summer I went to Japan for a month for vacation and it was one of the best experiences of my life! Throughout my trip in Japan, I visited Tokyo, Kyoto, Osaka, Kobe, and Shikoku Island. They were all amazing and unforgettable experiences. Therefore, I am very excited to share my journey as well as some recommendation spots on this website!

One of my highlights of Tokyo was visiting Tokyo Disneyland. Even as an average Disney show watcher, I found Disneyland to be a fascinating experience. The architecture and food was amazing and I would totally recommend. I loved all the rides there. However, if you are going espically during busy times, definetly invest in a express pass.

Shortly after Tokyo was Kyoto and Kyoto was known for more of the traditional temples. It was interesting seeing all the temples and shrines as well as take pictures in a kimono! To everyone who is interested in taking pictures or leaving memories, I would definetly recommend renting kimonos. It is reasonable price and there are so many options. Some even come with doing your hair and makeup! Other than that, I would say the matcha at Kyoto is to die for. In the Gion district, there is so many matcha and tradiitonal confectionary places that are amazing!

Lastly, Osaka! Osaka was a major hub for incredible food and night markets. I remember the okonomiyaki and takoyaki was to die for. In additon, shopping at Don Quijote was unlike the other cities. In the Dotonburi district, there was at least 7 floors os hopping and ferris wheel you can go on to see the river and city view. Overall, I think Osaka is a great place to hang around especially at night in arcades!
